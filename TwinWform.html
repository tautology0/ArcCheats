Twinworld saved game file format

offset use
?0Level
?1Parachutes
?2Springs
?3Ball Enlargers
?4Red
?5Green
?6Blue
!7Score
?11Lives
!12Amount of shots fired
!16Hit rate
!20Times died
?24*unknown*
?25Amount of shop calls
!26Flag to mark Key
!30Price of Key
!34Flag to mark Balls
!38Price of Balls
!42Flag to mark Spring
!46Price of Spring
!50Flag to mark Extra Life
!54Price of Extra Life
!58Flag to mark Parachute
!62Price of Parachute
!66Flag to mark Invunerability
!70Price of Invunerability
In the case of flags in the word <> 0 then the flag is true
